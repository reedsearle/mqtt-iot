User Story

As an instructor, I want to teach students how to use MQTT with their IoT devices but I am frustrated with the free options available.

Use Case

Ivan has access to the new MQTT broker and wants to try it out.

Preconditions

Ivan has an IoT device built to transmit data to the MQTT broker.

Ivan has his browser open to the MQTT broker login page.

Post Conditions

Ivan uses the MQTT broker with his students.

Interaction Flow

Ivan creates a login profile on the MQTT broker.

Ivan creates a test project.

Ivan adds feeds to his test project.

Ivan creates a dashboard for his test project.
